Dr. Wensheng Ganis currently a visiting scholar at Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Chicago, IL, USA, under the supervision by Prof. Philip S. Yu (ACM fellow and IEEE fellow). He received the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), Guangdong, China, in 2015 and 2019, respectively. His research interests include data mining, utility mining, and big data technologies. He has published more than 50 research papers in referred journals and international conferences, which have received more than 700 citations, as of 2019/12/31. A survey paper on utiltiy minining:

[1] Wensheng Gan et al. A Survey of Utility-Oriented Pattern Mining. IEEE TKDE 2019, or arXiv 2018. (A Tutorial)

Utility mining on transaction data

  • Dynamic utiltiy mining (survey paper): such as incremential, decremential and modificatail UPM.
  • Uncertain utility mining: e.g., PHUI-UP (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), PHUI-list (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), and MUHUI (2017 SOCO, Lin et al.) algorithms!
  • New utility measure namely utility occupancy: , 2018 TYCB, Gan et al.!

Utility mining on sequence data

  • High-utiltiy sequential pattern mining (survey paper): such as incremential, decremential and modificatail UPM.
  • ProUM algorithm (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), PHUI-list (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), and MUHUI (2017 SOCO, Lin et al.) algorithms!
  • HUSP-ULL algorithm: , 2018 TYCB, Gan et al.!

Utility mining on complex event sequences

  • High-utiltiy episode mining (survey paper): such as incremential, decremential and modificatail UPM.
  • UMEpi algorithm (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), PHUI-list (2016 KBS, Lin et al.), and MUHUI (2017 SOCO, Lin et al.) algorithms!
  • New utility measure namely utility occupancy: , 2018 TYCB, Gan et al.!

Utility computing is a process of acquisition, integration, and analysis of big and heterogeneous data generated by a diversity of sources.... Utility computing connects unobtrusive and ubiquitous fusion, indexing, mining and computing technologies, advanced data management and analytics models, and novel visualization methods, to XXXXX environment, human life quality, and city operation systems. A survey paper on urban computing:

Wensheng Gan, Jerry Chun‐Wei Lin, Vincent S Tseng, Philip S Yu. Utility Computing: concepts, methodologies, and applications. ACM Transaction on XXX (ACM TDS). 2019.

Utility computing is also a research project in IKElab Research, led by Dr. Wensheng Gan since August 2013. By analyzing the big data generated with rich information, a series of utility computing applications have been enabled as follows. One of core research problems is to fuse data across different domains. The other is to learn knowledge from spatio-temporal data, e.g. trajectories.

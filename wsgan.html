About me

I am currently an Associate Professor at Department of Computer Science, Jinan University (JNU). Before joining JNU in 2013, I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2013, supervised by Prof. Weijia JIA. Before that, I received the B.Eng. from Shandong University (SDU) in 2007 and M.Eng. from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2009.

Research Interests

I have broad interests in data science, with focuses on the following topics: database, data mining, uility mining, cloud computing, data & resource management, distributed systems as well as wireless networking.
主要从事计算机网络和云计算方面的系统应用及研究，特别是云计算网络、软件定义网络（SDN）、网络功能虚拟化（NFV）、边缘云计算（MEC）、无线网络等。
